About the Clinic

A New Standard in Non-Operative Orthopedic Care


OC Musculoskeletal Medical Center was founded on a simple belief: most orthopedic pain does not require surgery. Under the care of Dr. Ashkan Salamatipour — a fellowship-trained sports medicine physician — we offer the most advanced non-operative treatments available, performed with precision ultrasound guidance.

Every procedure at our clinic is guided by diagnostic ultrasound, ensuring unmatched accuracy in both diagnosis and treatment delivery. This is the same standard of care used in elite professional sports medicine — and it is now the standard for every patient who walks through our door.

Whether you are an active adult managing a nagging injury, an athlete looking to recover without surgery, or someone living with chronic joint pain after a failed procedure — we have a treatment pathway designed for you.

Treatments & Procedures

Cutting-Edge Therapies,
All Ultrasound-Guided


We offer the most advanced non-operative treatments in orthopedic medicine — the same therapies used with professional and collegiate athletes — performed with diagnostic ultrasound for precision and safety.

01

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)

A regenerative therapy using concentrated platelets from your own blood to stimulate healing in tendons, ligaments, muscles, and joints.

02

Alpha-2-Macroglobulin (A2M)

A naturally occurring protein that inhibits cartilage-degrading enzymes. An emerging regenerative option for osteoarthritis and joint preservation.

03

Peptide Therapy

Targeted peptide protocols that support tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and promote musculoskeletal recovery at the cellular level.

04

Microfragmented Adipose Tissue (MFAT)

A minimally processed autologous fat graft technique that delivers regenerative cells to damaged joint tissue using your body's own biology.

05

Prolotherapy

Injection-based treatment using a dextrose solution to stimulate the body's natural repair response in chronically painful ligaments and tendons.

06

Minimally Invasive Tenotomy

A precise needle-based procedure to break up and remove degenerative tendon tissue, promoting healing without open surgery.

07

SPRINT Peripheral Nerve Stimulation (PNS)

A temporary implanted lead delivers mild electrical pulses to peripheral nerves, providing sustained pain relief for chronic musculoskeletal conditions.

08

IOVERA° Cryoneuromodulation

A non-pharmacological neuromodulation technology that uses targeted cold therapy to temporarily disrupt pain-signaling nerves — without medication.

Every procedure at OC Musculoskeletal Medical Center is performed under real-time ultrasound guidance. This means diagnoses are confirmed visually before treatment, injections are delivered to the exact target structure, and outcomes are optimized through precision — not approximation.

About Dr. Salamatipour

Trained at the Best.
Caring for Every Patient.


Dr. Ashkan Salamatipour is a board-certified sports medicine and family medicine physician who completed his fellowship training at Indiana University — home to one of the nation's most respected sports medicine programs and the medical team behind the Hoosiers' national championship football team.

His expertise lies in ultrasound-guided diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal and orthopedic conditions. Every injection, every procedure, and every diagnosis at OCMSK is informed by real-time imaging — a standard Dr. Salamatipour carried from elite collegiate athletics into private practice.

Beyond his clinical work, Dr. Salamatipour is deeply committed to academic medicine. He has published numerous peer-reviewed research papers, serves as an ad hoc peer reviewer for multiple medical journals, and holds a faculty appointment as a clinical preceptor, training the next generation of physicians and medical students across several institutions.
